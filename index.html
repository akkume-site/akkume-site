The proposal

Akkume proposes a pilot that introduces a fixed-supply digital asset reserve layer into mortgage finance. The goal is not speculation. The goal is stronger credit structure, better alignment across the stack, and a more resilient housing finance system.

  • Support a pilot for reserve-layer mortgage enhancement
  • Build support for rule and standards changes
  • Create a coalition of borrowers, lenders, investors, servicers, and regulators
